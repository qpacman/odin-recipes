Lasagne!

Description

Ingredients

  • Ragu
  • Minced meat
  • Vegetables
  • Olive oil
  • Broth
  • Tomato sauce
  • Milk
  • Salt
  • Pasta
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Bechamel
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Salt
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Fry the meat, add vegetables
  2. Add some broth, the tomato sauce and bring it to simmer
  3. Add some more broth, keep it simmering
  4. When it is thick, set aside
  5. Use eggs, flour and salt, to make a dough
  6. Divide the dough into pieces, and roll them flat
  7. Melt butter, and while whisking, add flour and milk
  8. Preheat the oven
  9. Take a large dish, and layer ragu, then pasta, the bechamel. Repeat, until all run out. Put cheese on top
  10. Cook it in the oven
